Liquid Body Wash Recipe

Ingredient Phase Function Percentage Weight (g) Volume (mL) Temperature/Timing Check
Sodium Coco Isothionate (SCI) Phase A Primary surfactant for cleansing and lather 6% 90 - Heat to ~70°C (158°F), mix until dissolved (30-45 min)
Cocamidopropyl Betaine Phase A Secondary surfactant, enhances foam 30% 450 ~450 Heat together with SCI
Distilled Water Phase B Solvent, base for hydration 52.5% 787.5 787.5 Room temperature or pre-heated to ~50°C (122°F)
Glycerin Phase B Humectant, retains moisture 10% 150 150 Mix into water before combining phases
Liquid Germall Plus Phase B Preservative to prevent spoilage 0.5% 7.5 ~7.5 Add at room temperature after combining
Fragrance Oil (optional) Phase B Adds scent 1% 15 ~15 Add after cooling to below 40°C (104°F)

1. Prepare Phase A by combining SCI and Cocamidopropyl Betaine and heating until dissolved.

2. Prepare Phase B by mixing distilled water and glycerin.

3. Combine cooled Phase A and Phase B, then add preservative.

4. Test pH and adjust if necessary.

5. Optionally, add fragrance oil and color.

- Always ensure all tools are sterilized to avoid contamination.

- Allow the mixture to rest overnight for optimal results.

- Use high-quality fragrance oils and colorants to maintain product clarity.
